Lovely Runner

Lovely Runner is a romantic fantasy drama about a devoted fan who travels back in time to save her favorite idol from a tragic fate.

Episodes

16 Episodes

Cast

  • Byeon Woo Seok
  • Kim Hye Yoon
  • Song Geon Hee
  • Lee Seung Hyub

Synopsis

Im Sol (Kim Hye Yoon) is a passionate fan whose life was saved by the comforting words of idol Ryu Sun Jae (Byeon Woo Seok) during a difficult time. Years later, when she hears the shocking news of his sudden death, she is devastated.

Miraculously, Im Sol finds herself transported back to 2008 when Sun Jae was still a high school student. Determined to change his tragic future, she decides to stay close to him and protect him from the events that may lead to his fate.

As they spend more time together, their lives become deeply connected in unexpected ways.
