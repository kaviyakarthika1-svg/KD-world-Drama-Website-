True Beauty

True Beauty is a romantic comedy drama about a high school girl who becomes popular after mastering the art of makeup to hide her insecurities.

Episodes

16 Episodes

Cast

  • Moon Ga Young
  • Cha Eun Woo
  • Hwang In Yeop
  • Park Yoo Na

Synopsis

Lim Ju Kyung (Moon Ga Young) is a high school student who struggles with low self-esteem because of her appearance. After learning makeup skills through online tutorials, she transforms herself into a stunning beauty and quickly becomes popular at her new school.

Lee Su Ho (Cha Eun Woo), a quiet and handsome student, is the only person who knows her true appearance and becomes her secret ally. Meanwhile, Han Seo Jun (Hwang In Yeop), a rebellious yet kind-hearted student, develops feelings for Ju Kyung.

As friendships grow and secrets are revealed, Ju Kyung learns that true beauty comes from confidence and self-acceptance.
