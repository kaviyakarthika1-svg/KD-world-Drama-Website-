The K2

The K2 is an action romance drama about a former soldier who becomes a bodyguard for a powerful political family while uncovering secrets and protecting the hidden daughter of a presidential candidate.

Episodes

16 Episodes

Cast

  • Ji Chang Wook
  • Im Yoon Ah (Yoona)
  • Song Yoon Ah
  • Jo Sung Ha

Synopsis

Kim Je Ha (Ji Chang Wook), also known as K2, is a former mercenary soldier who becomes a bodyguard for a powerful presidential candidate's family.

He is hired by Choi Yoo Jin (Song Yoon Ah), the ambitious wife of the candidate, who uses him as a loyal weapon for her own political goals.

Meanwhile, Go Anna (Im Yoon Ah) is the secret daughter of the presidential candidate who has been hidden away from the world due to political scandals. Lonely and vulnerable, she forms a special bond with Je Ha, who becomes her protector.

As political conspiracies, betrayal, and danger surround them, Je Ha must fight powerful enemies while protecting Anna and seeking justice for the wrongs done in his past.
