Love Scout

Love Scout is a romantic drama that follows the story of two people working in the competitive corporate world who slowly discover love while helping each other grow both personally and professionally.

Episodes

12 Episodes

Cast

  • Han Ji Min
  • Lee Jun Hyuk
  • Kim Do Hoon
  • Kim Yoon Hye

Synopsis

Kang Ji Yoon (Han Ji Min) is a talented but work-focused CEO of a headhunting company who dedicates all her time to her career. She struggles with balancing her personal life while leading a successful business.

Yoo Eun Ho (Lee Jun Hyuk) is a skilled and kind secretary who is extremely organized and supportive in both work and life. Despite his calm personality, he carries his own emotional challenges.

When their professional lives become closely connected, they begin to rely on each other. Through their daily work, they slowly build trust, friendship, and eventually romantic feelings.

As they face workplace pressures and personal struggles, their journey shows how love can grow unexpectedly between two people who support each other's dreams.
