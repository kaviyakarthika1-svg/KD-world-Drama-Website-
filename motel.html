Motel California

Motel California is a romantic drama about two people who reunite years after being separated by life circumstances and rediscover their feelings for each other.

Episodes

12 Episodes

Cast

  • Lee Se Young
  • Na In Woo
  • Choi Min Soo
  • Kim Tae Hoon

Synopsis

Ji Kang Hee (Lee Se Young) grew up in a small town where her father ran a motel called “Motel California.” Because of her mixed background, she often felt like an outsider and eventually left her hometown to pursue her dreams in the city.

Years later, she returns to the town she once left behind and reconnects with her first love, Chun Yeon Soo (Na In Woo). As they spend time together again, old memories resurface and unresolved emotions begin to grow.

While facing personal struggles, family issues, and the challenges of their past, the two must decide whether they are ready to open their hearts again and find love where it all first began.
