A Week Before I Die

Romance • Fantasy • Melodrama


Synopsis

Twenty four-year-old Jung Hee Wan (Kim Min Ha) lives as a recluse, having lost her will to live. Suddenly, she receives a visit from her childhood friend and first love, Kim Ram Woo (Gong Myoung).

However, this is no ordinary visit. Ram Woo is now a grim reaper after he died six years ago, and he has come to tell Hee Wan that she only has one week left to live.

With Ram Woo's encouragement, Hee Wan creates a bucket list of things to do in her final days. Together, they begin fulfilling her last wishes.

Adapted from the novel "A Week Before I Die" by Seo Eun Chae.
