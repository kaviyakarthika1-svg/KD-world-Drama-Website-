Family By Choice

Family By Choice is a heartwarming drama about three people who grow up together like siblings even though they are not related by blood.

Episodes

16 Episodes

Cast

  • Hwang In Yeop
  • Jung Chae Yeon
  • Bae Hyun Sung

Synopsis

Kim San Ha (Hwang In Yeop), Yoon Joo Won (Jung Chae Yeon), and Kang Hae Jun (Bae Hyun Sung) grow up together under the same roof, supported by their loving parents. Although they are not related by blood, they form a strong bond and consider each other family. After spending their childhood together, circumstances lead them to go their separate ways. Ten years later, they reunite and rediscover the deep connection they once shared. As old memories resurface, they must navigate their complicated emotions, friendships, and the meaning of family. Through laughter, love, and challenges, the three friends learn that family is not always defined by blood but by the people who choose to stand by you.
