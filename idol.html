Idol I

Idol I is a romantic comedy drama that follows the journey of young idols as they chase their dreams in the competitive entertainment industry while discovering friendship, love, and personal growth.

Episodes

12 Episodes

Cast

  • Kim Ji Hoon
  • Lee Eun Sang
  • Park Ji Hoon
  • Choi Yujin

Synopsis

The drama centers on aspiring idols who work tirelessly to succeed in the highly competitive world of K-pop. As trainees struggle with intense training, public expectations, and personal challenges, they begin to build strong friendships and discover what it truly means to pursue their dreams. Amid the pressure of fame and the entertainment industry, unexpected romantic feelings begin to grow between some of the members. They must balance their careers, relationships, and ambitions while proving their talent to the world. Through hardships, performances, and emotional moments, the group learns that success is not only about fame but also about passion, teamwork, and believing in themselves.
