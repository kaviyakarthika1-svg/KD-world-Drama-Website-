To My Beloved Thief

To My Beloved Thief is a romantic historical drama that follows the unexpected love story between a clever thief and a noble woman during a turbulent period in history.

12 Episodes

Cast

Synopsis

Set in a historical era filled with political conflict and social differences, the story follows a mysterious and talented thief who steals from the wealthy while hiding his true identity.

During one of his missions, he crosses paths with a strong-willed young woman from a noble family. Despite coming from completely different worlds, the two begin to develop an unexpected bond.

As secrets, danger, and political intrigue surround them, they must navigate their growing feelings while facing powerful enemies and protecting the people they care about.

Their journey becomes a story of love, sacrifice, and courage as they challenge the barriers between their worlds.
