Way Back Love

Way Back Love is a romantic supernatural drama about two people who get a second chance at love when fate gives them an unexpected opportunity to revisit their past.

Episodes

12 Episodes

Cast

  • Kim Min Ha
  • Gong Myung
  • Oh Woo Ri
  • Ko Chang Seok

Synopsis

Jung Hee Wan lives a quiet life but carries painful memories from her past, especially about her first love who disappeared from her life years ago.

One day, something mysterious happens that allows her to revisit moments from the past. Through these experiences, she begins to understand the truth about what really happened and the feelings that were left unresolved.

As past and present begin to intertwine, Hee Wan must decide whether she can change her destiny and rediscover the love she once lost.
